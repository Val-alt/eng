Present Simple

Простое настоящие - повторяющее действие, рутина, расписание

Утверждение:

I / We / You / They + V

She / He / It + V + s (es)

I dream — He dreams

Отрицание:

I / We / You / They + do not (don’t) + V

She / He / It + does not (doesn’t) + V

I don’t go to school every day — They don’t drink beer

Вопрос:

Do + I / we / you / they + V ?

Does + she / he / it + V ?

Do you like pizza? — Does he live in Spain?
