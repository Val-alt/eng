Present Simple

Простое настоящие - повторяющее действие, рутина, расписание

Утверждение:

I / We / You / They + V

She / He / It + V + s (es)

I dream — He dreams

Отрицание:

I / We / You / They + do not (don’t) + V

She / He / It + does not (doesn’t) + V

I don’t go to school every day — They don’t drink beer

Вопрос:

Do + I / we / you / they + V ?

Does + she / he / it + V ?

Do you like pizza? — Does he live in Spain?

Present Continuous

Простое настоящие - в данный момент!

Утверждение:

I + am + Ving

She / He / It + is Ving

We / You / They + are + Ving

I am dreaming — He is dreaming — They are dreaming

Отрицание:

I + am + not + Ving

She / He / It + is + not + Ving

We / You / They + are + not + Ving

I am not going to school — She is not sleeping — They are not drinking beer

Вопрос:

Am + I + Ving ?

Is + she / he / it + Ving ?

Are + I + Ving ?

Am I reading a book? — Is he living in Spain? — Are you eating pizza?

Исключения!!!

run - running

swim - swimming

travel - travelling

lie - lying

die - dying
